Just Mathematics

Just Mathematics is a website I designed and built for an NGO that celebrates and rewards outstanding mathematics students. The platform makes it easy to discover scholarship programs, award categories, and success stories, while giving schools and sponsors a clear way to support young talent.

Live Link

Project Overview

The goal of Just Mathematics is to give an NGO a professional online presence where they can highlight their mission, showcase award programs, and communicate clearly with students, parents, and partners. I was responsible for turning their idea into a simple, welcoming website that feels trustworthy and easy to navigate.

The homepage introduces the organization and its focus on rewarding excellence in mathematics. Dedicated sections explain how the awards work, eligibility criteria, and how students can participate. I also created space to feature previous winners and impact stories, helping to build credibility and inspire new applicants.

From a technical perspective, I focused on a clean, responsive layout so that the site works well on both mobile and desktop. Clear typography, consistent spacing, and strong calls to action guide visitors to learn more, contact the NGO, or start the application process.
